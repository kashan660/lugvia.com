Chicago Moving Tips

  • Weather Planning: Avoid winter moves (Dec-Feb) due to harsh weather and snow
  • Parking Permits: Secure moving truck permits from the city 2-3 days in advance
  • High-Rise Moves: Book elevator reservations early for downtown buildings
  • Traffic Timing: Avoid rush hours (7-9 AM, 4-7 PM) and Cubs/Sox game days
  • Lake Effect: Plan for potential weather delays near the lakefront
