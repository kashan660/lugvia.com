Frequently Asked Questions About Packers and Movers

Get answers to the most common questions about our packers and movers services. Still have questions? Our experts are here to help!

Pricing & Costs

How much do packers and movers cost?

Moving costs vary based on distance, size, and services:

  • Local moves: $80-120 per hour for 2-3 movers
  • Long-distance moves: $2,500-$5,000 depending on distance
  • Packing services: $500-$1,500 for full-service packing
  • Storage: $50-$300 per month

Get free quotes through Lugvia to compare prices from licensed packers and movers.

What factors affect moving costs?

Several factors influence your moving costs:

  • Distance of the move
  • Size and weight of belongings
  • Time of year (peak season costs more)
  • Additional services (packing, storage, assembly)
  • Accessibility of pickup and delivery locations
  • Insurance coverage level

Booking & Scheduling

How far in advance should I book packers and movers?

Recommended booking timeline:

  • Peak season (May-September): 6-8 weeks in advance
  • Off-peak season: 4-6 weeks in advance
  • Last-minute moves: Lugvia can find movers within 24-48 hours
  • Holiday periods: Book 8-10 weeks ahead

Early booking ensures better availability and often better rates.

Can I get same-day moving services?

Yes! While advance booking is recommended, Lugvia specializes in finding last-minute packers and movers. Same-day services may be available for:

  • Local moves within the same city
  • Small apartment or studio moves
  • Emergency relocations

Contact us immediately for urgent moving needs.

Packing Services

What's included in full-service packing?

Full-service packing includes:

  • Professional packers bring all supplies
  • Careful packing of all household items
  • Special handling for fragile items
  • Labeling and inventory of all boxes
  • Unpacking services at destination (optional)
  • Disposal of packing materials

This service saves you time and ensures professional protection of your belongings.

Are my belongings insured during the move?

Yes, licensed packers and movers provide basic liability coverage (60 cents per pound). For full replacement value protection, purchase additional moving insurance. Lugvia partners with insured, licensed movers for your peace of mind.

Moving Day

What should I expect on moving day?

On moving day, expect:

  • Movers arrive within the scheduled time window
  • Walk-through and inventory of items
  • Careful loading and protection of furniture
  • Regular updates on progress
  • Final inventory check at destination
  • Payment upon completion

Our packers and movers are trained professionals who handle your belongings with care.

What items cannot be moved by packers and movers?

Movers cannot transport hazardous materials like:

  • Paint, propane, ammunition, chemicals
  • Perishable food items
  • Plants (for long-distance moves)
  • Pets
  • Important documents (carry personally)
  • Jewelry and valuables (carry personally)

Check with your mover for a complete prohibited items list.
