Essential Moving Tips

Pro tips to make your move smoother and more efficient

Label Everything

Label boxes with contents and destination room. Use color-coded labels for easy identification.

Take Photos

Photograph valuable items and electronics setup before packing for insurance and reassembly.

Pack Essentials Box

Pack a "first day" box with toiletries, medications, clothes, and important documents.

Don't Overpack

Keep boxes under 50 pounds. Use smaller boxes for heavy items like books.

Protect Valuables

Transport jewelry, important documents, and irreplaceable items yourself.

Start Early

Begin packing non-essential items 6-8 weeks before your move date.
