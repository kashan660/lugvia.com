Moving in the Live Music Capital

Essential insights for your Austin move - from festival seasons to neighborhood guides

🎸

Austin Moving Tips

  • Avoid SXSW (March) and ACL (October) for easier moves
  • Book early during UT semester changes
  • Consider heat protection for summer moves
  • Plan around major events and festivals
  • Check for street closures during events
📅

Best Moving Times

  • Best Months: November-February (cooler weather)
  • Best Days: Tuesday-Thursday (less traffic)
  • Best Time: Early morning (6-8 AM start)
  • Avoid: Festival weekends and UT move-in
  • Weather: Hot summers, mild winters
🏛️

Iconic Austin Areas

  • Downtown: Business district, entertainment
  • South Austin: Keep Austin Weird culture
  • East Austin: Hip neighborhoods, food scene
  • Zilker: Park living, ACL festival grounds
  • The Domain: Upscale shopping and dining
